What counts as legacy here

The repository also contains older exploratory non-v0 generators: Move, Route, Monte Carlo, and beam-search experiments under scripts/lib. They are useful history, but the current legacy/new compiler comparison is the v0 pair: scripts/v0/compile.ts and scripts/v0/optimizer/api.ts.
